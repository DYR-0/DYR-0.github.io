About

I am a PhD student in Myers-Lawson School of Construction at Virginia Tech, where I focus on disaster resilience and urban computing. My advisor is Dr. Lu Zhang I got my master at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where I worked on a indoor occupancy detection project with Dr. Zhe Wang.
